Arrangement Commissions

Currently, I open a public commissions for instrumental arrangement according to your needs. The process usually take 2 till 14 days.

Pricing

There are 3 standard packages for you to choose.

  • USD 25 / IDR 325.000 - Instrumental for TV-Size/short cover (duration up to 2 minutes)
  • USD 45 / IDR 600.000 - Instrumental for full-lenght cover (duration more than 2 minutes)
  • USD 300 / IDR 4.000.000 - I will make instrumental arrangement for your original song (recommended genre: Pop, Rock, and Metal)
  • - I also open a commissions for drum arrangement and vocal processing (ex: timing, tuning, etc.), just slide into my DM's for more details. -

NOTES

  • * All packages includes instrument mixing & mastering
  • * All packages has a maximum of 2 revisions
  • * Arrangement for cover song not include add-ons (such as changing mood of the song, etc.)
  • * All packages are already for commercial use
  • * All prices can be negotiated depends on the difficulty of the song
